Computer Hardware

Computer hardware refers to all of the physical components that go into the functioning of a computer. These can include external components: mouse, keyboard, monitor etc, or internal components: hard drive, RAM, motherboard, CPU, or GPU. Over the years hardware has evolved and gained immense amounts of power while becoming increasingly compact. Each unit of the computer comes together to perform a specific function in order to work in the way that we expect. The main units of hardware covered in the site are the CPU, the brain of the computer, the GPU, the device that makes the visuals possible, and the storage units, hard drives and solid state drives that store information.
